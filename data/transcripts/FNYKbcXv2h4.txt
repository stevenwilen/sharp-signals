Was Ronda Rousey the most overhyped
fighter in UFC history? Let's look at
the facts.
Ronda was a pioneer, no doubt, but she
built a 12-win streak against a division
that was still in its infancy.
The media told us she was an elite
striker. Her coach even claimed that she
could beat world-class boxer.
But when the game evolved, Ronda didn't.
She ran into Holly Holm, a real striker.
The invincible aura shattered.
Then came Amanda Nunes. 48 seconds of
standing still while getting tagged. No
head movement, no adjustments. Great
pioneer? Absolutely. The goat? Not even
close. She was a specialist in a world
that became mixed. Is Ronda overrated or
just a victim of her own hype? Let me
know in the comments.
