Instead of purely focusing on damage,

directly hurt your opponent, but it
makes them second guessess themselves.
It tilts the judging optics and it
creates an energy in the arena that can
carry you through a close fight. In a
sport where razor thin fights are common
and bad judging is even more common,
perception can sometimes be more
