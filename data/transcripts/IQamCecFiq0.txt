Then came a naba in Cape Town. First
decision in PFL. A fight that showed her
composure, sure, but also gave her a
souvenir she didn't ask for. A broken
hand. And that's where all of this
really starts. Because once you've been
out with something like that, once
you've had to trust your body to come
back, trust that your money maker weapon
still does what it's supposed to do, you
don't just roll right into the scariest
stylistic matchups available. You take a
fight that lets you test things. Touch
distance. Feel timing. Land clean.
