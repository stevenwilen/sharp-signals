This is what makes Jon Silva one of the
most dangerous strikers in the UFC. When
Jordane blitzes Silva the first time, he
notices that Jordane's right hand drops
and counters with a lead hook, but has
his weight too far backwards to land
clean. When Jordane blitzes again, he
adjusts and plans after evading to drop
Jordane. When Silva faints the 3-2
against Melik, Melik reacts by throwing
a lead hook and briefly mispositioning
his rear hand. So next time, instead of
evading the hook, Silva accepts the
possibility of eating it and blasts the
two, knocking on Melik. Silva's constant
reads and small adjustments let him turn
the smallest opportunity into a
knockout.
