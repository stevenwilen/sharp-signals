Round one opens and right out of the
gate, the dynamics are interesting.
Gain's jab and leg kick combinations are
making noise. He opens Aspenol's nose.
We're already seeing damage. Aspenol is
pressing forward, trying to impose his
power, his threat looming. For a moment,
you sense the narrative shift. Game
might be the one dictating. And then at
4 minutes and 35 seconds into the round,
boom. The moment you'll see replay for
years, Gain throws what looks like a
kick. Aspenol drops his guard to block.
Gain's left hand drifts and his fingers
connect. Both eyes knuckle deep. That's
not a fuzzy contact accident. That's a
brutal visual. Aspenol immediately
signals he can't see. The ref, Jason
Herszog, halts. The doctor goes in. The
camp begins.
