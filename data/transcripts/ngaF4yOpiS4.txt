Which fighter has had more successful
career, Alexander Volkanovski or Israel
Adesanya?
Man, this is a tough one. I got to go
with Israel Adesanya. I always look at a
career and see the chances that somebody
has taken to become the best in the
world. When Israel was at middleweight,
bro, he fought everybody. In particular,
that fight that he had with Kelvin
Gastelum, the Whittaker fight, for the
Romero fight, Paulo Costa fight. I think
the longevity of it and then beating
Alex Pereira to avenge that loss. I
would go Adesanya. You clear out the
whole division. You go up, challenge for
that title, come back down, and you try
going through the division again.
