How do I break down a fight with Islam
and Tapora? Stylistically, I don't think
it's grappler against striker. Islam
showed his last couple fights that he is
comfortable on the feet. He's fought the
shorter guy, Vulcanowski. He knocked him
out with a head kick. And then the
Dustin fight, he showed his boxing with
Tapora, who does have really good
grappling, really good wrestling. He's
going to want to push Islam backwards
and he really good with his fainting. He
doesn't really throw a lot of kicks. On
the other side, Islam is really good
with his kicks. Islam is not going to be
in that boxing range unless he's
grabbing you. Islam has the best
striking defense in the UFC. I think
he's not going to stand there and and
trade with you. If he is in that range,
he's going to grab you.
