I'm a big Josh Barnett fan. When I was
preparing for Carlos Condit, I went down
to train at Clovis. Clovis is where my
high school wrestling coach coaches now,
and that's where Josh Barnett came from.
So, Josh Barnett comes in the wrestling
room, 20 years old, and I'm starting to
wrestle this guy. I didn't know how good
he was. I didn't realize this guy's a
three-time state champ. He's going to go
on to All-American in college, going to
go on to play in the NFL. I didn't
realize what I was dealing with. Josh
Barnett definitely didn't beat the crap
out of me too bad, but definitely got
the best of me in the wrestling
training. Cut my head up a little bit,
and at the time, he's a very, very
humble, nice kid on the other side of
the camera.
